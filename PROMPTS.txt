Final Rewritten & Complete System Requirements

Add functionality for residents to access the system.
Resident accounts will have limited access, restricted only to:

Viewing and updating their User Account information

Viewing and updating their Resident Profile record

Submitting online document requests (e.g., certificates, permits, clearances)

Accessing barangay announcements

Viewing barangay events

Residents must not access any administrative or staff-only modules.

Residents should also be able to register online, with their registration requiring review and approval by an admin or authorized staff member before activation.

The system must also include:

Resident Reports Module

Generate demographic reports

Resident statistics

Population summaries

Household reports

Notifications System

Alerts for new online document requests

Alerts for new resident registration requests

Status updates sent to residents (approved, denied, completed)

Analytical Dashboard

Display key metrics such as:

Total residents

Pending and approved document requests

Active vs. inactive residents

Number of announcements and events

Weekly/monthly request trends

Population breakdown (age, gender, household count)

Visual charts, graphs, and summaries for admins and staff
